# Paper Plane / 纸飞机

**Difficulty / 难度：** ★★★☆☆

**Technique / 技法：** Shake / 摇和法

---

## History / 背景

The Paper Plane was created in 2007 by bartender Sam Ross at the legendary Milk & Honey bar in New York City. Ross was inspired by the M.I.A. song "Paper Planes" and sought to create a modern equal-parts cocktail in the tradition of the classic Last Word. The stroke of genius was incorporating Amaro Nonino Quintessentia — a refined Italian amaro — alongside Aperol and bourbon, balanced with fresh lemon juice. The result is a beautifully balanced cocktail that is simultaneously bitter, sweet, sour, and boozy without being overwhelming in any direction. The Paper Plane has since become a modern classic, cementing Sam Ross's reputation and introducing many drinkers to the world of amari.

纸飞机由调酒师 Sam Ross 于 2007 年在纽约传奇酒吧 Milk & Honey 创作。Ross 的灵感来源于 M.I.A. 的歌曲《Paper Planes》，他希望沿袭经典酒款"最后一言"（Last Word）的传统，创作一款现代等比例鸡尾酒。其神来之笔在于将 Amaro Nonino Quintessentia——一款精致的意大利苦味利口酒——与 Aperol 和波本威士忌搭配，再以鲜榨柠檬汁平衡。最终成品是一款平衡精妙的鸡尾酒，同时兼具苦、甜、酸和酒感，却不会在任何方向上过于突出。纸飞机此后成为一款现代经典，巩固了 Sam Ross 的声誉，也让许多饮者由此踏入了意大利苦味利口酒（Amari）的世界。

## Ingredients / 配料

| Ingredient / 配料 | ml | oz |
|---|---|---|
| Bourbon / 波本威士忌 | 22.5 ml | ¾ oz |
| Aperol / 阿佩罗 | 22.5 ml | ¾ oz |
| Amaro Nonino Quintessentia / 诺尼诺苦味利口酒 | 22.5 ml | ¾ oz |
| Fresh Lemon Juice / 鲜榨柠檬汁 | 22.5 ml | ¾ oz |

> **Note / 备注：** This is an equal-parts recipe — each ingredient is exactly ¾ oz (22.5 ml). This makes it easy to remember and scale.
> 这是一款等比例配方——每种配料均为 ¾ oz（22.5 ml）。便于记忆和按比例调整。

---

## Glassware / 酒杯

Coupe glass / 碟形杯

## Garnish / 装饰

No garnish (or optional lemon twist) / 无装饰（或可选柠檬皮卷）

---

## Instructions / 调制步骤

### English

1. Chill a coupe glass by filling it with ice and cold water; set aside.
2. Add 22.5 ml bourbon, 22.5 ml Aperol, 22.5 ml Amaro Nonino, and 22.5 ml fresh lemon juice into a cocktail shaker.
3. Fill the shaker with ice cubes.
4. Shake vigorously for 10–12 seconds until well chilled.
5. Discard the ice water from the chilled coupe glass.
6. Double-strain through a Hawthorne strainer and fine mesh sieve into the chilled coupe.
7. Serve as-is — the Paper Plane is traditionally served without a garnish. If desired, express a lemon twist over the surface and discard.

### 中文

1. 在碟形杯中放入冰块和冷水进行冰镇，放置一旁。
2. 将 22.5 ml 波本威士忌、22.5 ml 阿佩罗、22.5 ml 诺尼诺苦味利口酒和 22.5 ml 鲜榨柠檬汁倒入雪克壶中。
3. 在雪克壶中装入冰块。
4. 大力摇晃 10–12 秒，直至充分冰镇。
5. 倒掉冰镇杯中的冰水。
6. 使用霍桑隔冰器和细网筛双重过滤，将酒液滤入冰镇好的碟形杯中。
7. 直接上桌——纸飞机传统上不加任何装饰。如有需要，可在酒液表面拧挤一片柠檬皮后丢弃。

## Pro Tips / 专业小贴士

- **Equal parts is the key** — The beauty of this cocktail is its perfect balance. Resist the temptation to adjust ratios until you've tried it as written.
  **等比例是关键** —— 这款鸡尾酒的精妙之处在于完美的平衡。在按原方尝试之前，请抵住调整比例的诱惑。

- **Amaro Nonino is specific** — While other amari can work, Amaro Nonino Quintessentia's delicate grape-based character with notes of saffron and orange peel is what makes this cocktail sing. Generic "amaro" substitutions will yield a different drink.
  **诺尼诺苦味利口酒是特定选择** —— 虽然其他苦味利口酒也能使用，但 Amaro Nonino Quintessentia 以葡萄为基底、带有藏红花和橙皮风味的细腻特质，才是这款鸡尾酒的灵魂。使用普通"苦味酒"替代会得到截然不同的饮品。

- **Fresh lemon juice only** — Bottled lemon juice has a flat, oxidized taste. Always squeeze fresh.
  **只用鲜榨柠檬汁** —— 瓶装柠檬汁味道平淡且氧化。务必使用现榨。

- **Bourbon selection** — A mid-proof, not too oaky bourbon works best (e.g., Buffalo Trace, Four Roses). Avoid high-proof barrel-strength bourbons that can overpower the delicate balance.
  **波本选择** —— 中等酒精度、橡木味不过重的波本效果最佳（如 Buffalo Trace、Four Roses）。避免使用高度数桶强波本，以免破坏精妙的平衡。

## Common Variations / 常见变体

| Variation / 变体 | Description / 说明 |
|---|---|
| **Rye Paper Plane / 黑麦纸飞机** | Substitute bourbon with rye whiskey for a spicier, drier profile. / 用黑麦威士忌替代波本，口感更辛辣干爽。 |
| **Mezcal Paper Plane / 梅斯卡尔纸飞机** | Replace bourbon with mezcal for a smoky variation. / 用梅斯卡尔酒替代波本，增添烟熏风味。 |
| **Paper Plane with Cynar / 朝鲜蓟纸飞机** | Swap Amaro Nonino for Cynar for a more vegetal, earthy bitterness. / 用 Cynar 替代诺尼诺，获得更具草本泥土气息的苦味。 |
| **Paper Plane Spritz / 纸飞机气泡版** | Build over ice in a wine glass and top with prosecco. / 在酒杯中加冰构建，顶部注入普罗赛克气泡酒。 |
