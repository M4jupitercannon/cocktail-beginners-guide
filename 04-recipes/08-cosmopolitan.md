# 大都会 / Cosmopolitan

**难度 Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆

**技法 Technique:** 摇和法 Shake

---

## 背景 History

大都会（Cosmopolitan，常简称"Cosmo"）的现代版本由传奇调酒师 Dale DeGroff 于 1990 年代在纽约的 Rainbow Room 定型推广。虽然它的雏形可能更早出现在 1980 年代的旧金山或纽约，但真正让它风靡全球的是美剧《欲望都市》（Sex and the City）——Carrie Bradshaw 和朋友们人手一杯 Cosmo 的画面成为了流行文化的经典符号。这是一杯平衡精妙的鸡尾酒：伏特加的干净、橙皮酒的甜美、蔓越莓的酸甜和青柠的清爽完美融合。

The modern Cosmopolitan — commonly called a "Cosmo" — was refined and popularised by legendary bartender Dale DeGroff at the Rainbow Room in New York during the 1990s. While its origins may trace back to 1980s San Francisco or New York, it was the TV show *Sex and the City* that propelled it to global fame — Carrie Bradshaw holding a pink Cosmo became a pop-culture icon. It is an expertly balanced cocktail: clean vodka, sweet orange liqueur, tart cranberry, and fresh lime harmonise beautifully.

## 配料 Ingredients

| 原料 Ingredient | 用量 Amount | 盎司 oz |
|:---|:---:|:---:|
| 柑橘伏特加（或普通伏特加）Citrus vodka (or regular vodka) | 45 ml | 1½ oz |
| 君度 Cointreau | 15 ml | ½ oz |
| 鲜榨青柠汁 Fresh lime juice | 15 ml | ½ oz |
| 蔓越莓汁 Cranberry juice | 30 ml | 1 oz |

## 步骤 Instructions

### 中文步骤

1. 将柑橘伏特加、君度、青柠汁和蔓越莓汁加入摇壶。
2. 加满冰块，大力摇和 10–15 秒，直到摇壶外壁结霜。
3. 双重过滤（Hawthorne + Fine Strainer），滤入预先冰镇过的马天尼杯或碟形杯中。
4. 在杯口上方削一片橙皮，将有油脂的一面朝向酒液，用拇指和食指捏挤，让精油喷洒到酒面上（这个动作叫"火焰橙皮"时可以用火点燃，但不是必须的）。
5. 将橙皮轻搭在杯沿作为装饰。

### English Steps

1. Add citrus vodka, Cointreau, lime juice, and cranberry juice to a shaker.
2. Fill with ice and shake hard for 10–15 seconds until the shaker is frosty.
3. Double strain (Hawthorne strainer + fine mesh strainer) into a chilled Martini glass or coupe.
4. Cut a coin-shaped piece of orange peel over the glass. Hold it skin-side down over the drink and pinch firmly to express the oils onto the surface (optionally flame the peel for an aromatic flourish).
5. Rest the orange peel on the rim as garnish.

## 小贴士 Pro Tips

- **蔓越莓汁的选择：** 使用 100% 纯蔓越莓汁（Unsweetened）会使口感偏酸偏干，适合喜欢清爽口味的人。常见的 Ocean Spray 鸡尾酒蔓越莓汁（Cranberry Cocktail）含糖较多但更容易获得，二者皆可。
  **Choosing cranberry juice:** 100% unsweetened cranberry juice gives a drier, tart result. Ocean Spray cranberry cocktail is sweeter and more widely available. Both work — adjust sweetness accordingly.
- **颜色控制：** Cosmo 应该是淡粉色而非深红色。如果颜色太深，说明蔓越莓汁加多了。
  **Colour control:** A well-made Cosmo should be pale pink, not deep red. If it looks too dark, you've added too much cranberry.
- **柑橘伏特加 vs 普通伏特加：** Dale DeGroff 的经典配方使用 Absolut Citron。普通伏特加也完全可以，口感会更干净一些。
  **Citrus vs regular vodka:** Dale DeGroff's classic recipe calls for Absolut Citron. Regular vodka works perfectly well and gives a cleaner, more neutral profile.
- **预冷杯子很重要。** 这款鸡尾酒纯饮（up），没有冰块保持温度，所以杯子必须提前冰镇。
  **Chill your glass.** Served up with no ice, the drink warms quickly in a warm glass. Put it in the freezer for 10–15 minutes before use.

## 常见变体 Common Variations

| 变体 Variation | 说明 Description |
|:---|:---|
| **White Cosmopolitan / 白色大都会** | 用白蔓越莓汁代替红蔓越莓汁，成品近乎透明，口感更清爽。Use white cranberry juice for a nearly clear, more delicate drink. |
| **Metropolitan / 大都市** | 用黑加仑利口酒（Chambord 或 Crème de Cassis）替代蔓越莓汁，风味更浓郁深邃。Replace cranberry with blackcurrant liqueur (Chambord or Crème de Cassis) for a deeper, richer drink. |
| **Raspberry Cosmo / 覆盆子大都会** | 用覆盆子利口酒或鲜覆盆子泥搭配蔓越莓汁，增加果香层次。Add raspberry liqueur or fresh raspberry purée for a berry-forward twist. |
