# Daiquiri / 代基里

**Difficulty / 难度:** ★★☆☆☆

**Technique / 技法:** Shake / 摇和法

**Glassware / 杯具:** Coupe glass / 碟形香槟杯

**Garnish / 装饰:** Lime wheel or lime twist / 青柠片或青柠皮卷

---

## History / 背景

The Daiquiri takes its name from a beach and iron mine near Santiago de Cuba, where American mining engineer Jennings Cox is said to have first mixed rum, lime, and sugar around 1898. The drink soon traveled to Havana, where it became the signature cocktail of the legendary El Floridita bar — famously patronized by Ernest Hemingway. Despite being buried under decades of frozen, artificially flavored imitations, the classic Daiquiri remains one of the most respected cocktails among professional bartenders: a perfect test of balance between spirit, citrus, and sweetness.

代基里得名于古巴圣地亚哥附近的一处海滩和铁矿。据说约 1898 年，美国采矿工程师詹宁斯·考克斯在此首次将朗姆酒、青柠和糖混合调制。这款酒很快传到哈瓦那，成为传奇酒吧 El Floridita 的招牌鸡尾酒——海明威是那里的常客。尽管多年来被冰沙版和人工调味的仿品掩盖了光芒，经典代基里在专业调酒师心中始终是最受尊敬的鸡尾酒之一：它是烈酒、柑橘和甜度之间完美平衡的终极考验。

## Ingredients / 配料

| Ingredient / 原料 | ml | oz |
| --- | --- | --- |
| White rum / 白朗姆酒 | 60 ml | 2 oz |
| Fresh lime juice / 新鲜青柠汁 | 25 ml | 0.75 oz |
| Simple syrup (1:1) / 糖浆（1:1） | 15 ml | 0.5 oz |

---

## Instructions / 步骤

### English

1. Add 60 ml (2 oz) of white rum, 25 ml (0.75 oz) of freshly squeezed lime juice, and 15 ml (0.5 oz) of simple syrup to a cocktail shaker.
2. Fill the shaker with ice cubes.
3. Shake vigorously for 10–15 seconds until the shaker is frosty on the outside.
4. **Double strain** through both the Hawthorne strainer and a fine mesh strainer into a pre-chilled coupe glass. This removes ice shards and lime pulp for a silky texture.
5. Garnish with a lime wheel on the rim or a lime twist.
6. Serve immediately — this drink does not wait.

### 中文

1. 将 60 ml（2 oz）白朗姆酒、25 ml（0.75 oz）新鲜挤榨的青柠汁和 15 ml（0.5 oz）糖浆加入摇酒壶中。
2. 在摇酒壶中装满冰块。
3. 大力摇晃 10–15 秒，直到壶外壁结霜。
4. **双重过滤**——同时使用霍桑过滤器和细网过滤器，将酒液滤入预冷的碟形香槟杯中。这一步能去除碎冰和青柠果肉，使口感如丝般顺滑。
5. 用青柠片挂在杯口或青柠皮卷装饰。
6. 立即享用——这杯酒不能等。

## Pro Tips / 进阶技巧

- **Double straining is essential / 双重过滤至关重要：** The Daiquiri should be crystal clear and silky. Always double strain through a fine mesh strainer. 代基里应当清澈如水、口感丝滑，务必使用细网过滤器进行双重过滤。
- **Taste and adjust / 先尝后调：** Before shaking, taste the mixture with a straw. Adjust lime or syrup to hit the perfect sweet-sour balance for your palate. 摇晃前先用吸管尝味，根据个人口味调整青柠汁或糖浆的量，找到完美的酸甜平衡。
- **Rum selection matters / 朗姆酒的选择很重要：** A clean, dry white rum works best (Havana Club 3, Plantation 3 Stars, Banks 5 Island). Avoid overly sweet or spiced rums. 干净、干型的白朗姆最佳（如 Havana Club 3 年、Plantation 3 Stars、Banks 5 Island），避免过甜或加香料的朗姆酒。
- **Chill the coupe / 预冷碟形杯：** A warm glass will cause the drink to lose its chill immediately. Keep coupes in the freezer or fill with ice water while you prep. 温热的杯子会让酒迅速升温。提前将碟形杯放入冰箱或用冰水预冷。
- **Speed is everything / 速度就是一切：** Unlike build drinks, shaken cocktails begin dying the moment they leave the shaker. Prep your garnish before shaking. 与直调法不同，摇和类鸡尾酒一离开摇酒壶就开始"衰败"。请在摇晃前准备好装饰物。

## Common Variations / 常见变体

| Variation / 变体 | Description / 说明 |
| --- | --- |
| **Hemingway Daiquiri (Papa Doble)** | Add 15 ml maraschino liqueur and 15 ml fresh grapefruit juice; reduce or omit simple syrup. 加入 15 ml 黑樱桃利口酒和 15 ml 新鲜西柚汁，减少或去掉糖浆。 |
| **Strawberry Daiquiri (classic)** | Muddle 3–4 fresh strawberries in the shaker before adding other ingredients. Not the frozen slushy version. 在摇酒壶中捣碎 3–4 颗新鲜草莓后加入其他原料——不是冰沙版本。 |
| **Banana Daiquiri** | Add 30 ml banana liqueur and half a fresh banana; shake hard. 加入 30 ml 香蕉利口酒和半根新鲜香蕉，用力摇晃。 |
| **Aged Rum Daiquiri** | Swap white rum for aged rum (e.g., Appleton Estate, El Dorado 5) for deeper, more complex flavors. 用陈年朗姆酒替代白朗姆（如 Appleton Estate、El Dorado 5），风味更深邃复杂。 |
