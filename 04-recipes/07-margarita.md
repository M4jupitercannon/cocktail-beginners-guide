# 玛格丽特 / Margarita

**难度 Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆

**技法 Technique:** 摇和法 Shake

---

## 背景 History

玛格丽特是全球最受欢迎的龙舌兰鸡尾酒，起源众说纷纭——最广为流传的版本是 1948 年墨西哥 Tijuana 的调酒师 Carlos "Danny" Herrera 为一位只喝龙舌兰的女客人特调了此酒。它的核心配方（龙舌兰 + 橙皮利口酒 + 青柠汁）简洁完美，盐边的设计更是点睛之笔——盐能抑制苦味、增强甜感，让整杯酒风味层次分明。

The Margarita is the world's most popular tequila cocktail. Its exact origin is debated — one widely told story credits Carlos "Danny" Herrera, a bartender in Tijuana, Mexico, who created it around 1948 for a guest who would only drink tequila. Its core formula (tequila + orange liqueur + lime juice) is elegant in its simplicity. The iconic salt rim suppresses bitterness and enhances sweetness, adding another dimension to every sip.

## 步骤 Instructions

### 中文步骤

1. **盐边准备：** 取一小碟细盐。用青柠角擦湿杯口外侧的一半（半盐边），然后将杯口蘸入盐碟，轻轻旋转，抖落多余的盐。
2. 将龙舌兰、君度和青柠汁加入摇壶，加满冰块。
3. 大力摇和 10–15 秒，直到摇壶外壁结霜。
4. 滤入准备好盐边的杯中。如用古典杯，加入新鲜冰块；如用碟形杯，纯饮。
5. 以青柠轮片装饰。

### English Steps

1. **Salt rim:** Place fine salt on a small plate. Moisten the outside of half the glass rim with a lime wedge (half salt rim), then dip and rotate gently. Tap off excess salt.
2. Add tequila, Cointreau, and fresh lime juice to a shaker and fill with ice.
3. Shake hard for 10–15 seconds until the shaker is frosty.
4. Strain into the prepared glass. If using a rocks glass, add fresh ice; if using a coupe, serve up.
5. Garnish with a lime wheel.

## 小贴士 Pro Tips

- **只做半盐边。** 让客人自由选择每一口是否搭配盐味，这是专业调酒师的标准做法。
  **Use a half salt rim.** This lets the drinker choose a salty or salt-free sip — standard practice among professional bartenders.
- **盐粒不要太粗。** 粗盐容易掉落到酒中，破坏口感。推荐使用细海盐或玛格丽特专用盐。
  **Don't use coarse salt.** It falls into the drink and over-seasons it. Fine sea salt or Margarita salt works best.
- **龙舌兰的选择：** 初学者推荐 100% 龙舌兰（100% Agave）的 Blanco 款，避免使用 Mixto（混合龙舌兰），风味差距大。
  **Choose your tequila wisely:** Go for 100% agave Blanco tequila. Avoid Mixto (mixed) tequila — the quality difference is enormous.
- **冷冻版本（Frozen Margarita）：** 所有材料加冰放入搅拌机打碎即可，但这是另一种完全不同的体验。
  **Frozen Margarita:** Blend all ingredients with ice in a blender. A fun variation, but a very different experience.

## 常见变体 Common Variations

| 变体 Variation | 说明 Description |
|:---|:---|
| **Tommy's Margarita / 汤米玛格丽特** | 用龙舌兰糖浆（Agave Syrup）替代君度，口感更纯净。配方：60 ml tequila, 30 ml lime, 15 ml agave syrup。Replace Cointreau with agave syrup for a cleaner, agave-forward drink. |
| **Spicy Margarita / 辣味玛格丽特** | 在摇壶中加入 2–3 片新鲜辣椒（如 Jalapeño）一起摇和，或使用辣椒浸泡龙舌兰。Muddle 2–3 slices of fresh Jalapeño in the shaker, or use chilli-infused tequila. |
| **Mezcal Margarita / 梅斯卡尔玛格丽特** | 用梅斯卡尔替代部分或全部龙舌兰，带来烟熏风味。Swap some or all tequila for mezcal for a smoky twist. |
| **Cadillac Margarita / 凯迪拉克玛格丽特** | 使用顶级龙舌兰（如 Reposado）和 Grand Marnier 替代 Triple sec，风味更奢华。Use premium Reposado tequila and Grand Marnier for a luxurious upgrade. |
