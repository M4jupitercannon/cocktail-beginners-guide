## 1. Recommended Books

### Beginner Level

| Title | Author | Why Read It |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **The Craft of the Cocktail** | Dale DeGroff | The "King Cocktail's" classic primer — solid recipes, rich history |
| **The Bar Book: Elements of Cocktail Technique** | Jeffrey Morgenthaler | Focuses on technique details, from ice handling to shaking form |
| **12 Bottle Bar** | David Solmonson & Lesley Jacobs Solmonson | Hundreds of cocktails from just 12 bottles — budget-friendly approach |

### Intermediate Level

| Title | Author | Why Read It |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **Death & Co: Modern Classic Cocktails** | David Kaplan et al. | Recipe bible from NYC's legendary bar, modern classics defined |
| **Cocktail Codex** | Alex Day, Nick Fauchald, David Kaplan | Deconstructs all cocktails into 6 "root recipes" — best for understanding principles |
| **The PDT Cocktail Book** | Jim Meehan | From NYC's PDT bar, blending recipes with bar culture |
| **Smuggler's Cove** | Martin Cate | The ultimate Tiki cocktail guide |

### Advanced / Science-Focused

| Title | Author | Why Read It |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **Liquid Intelligence** | Dave Arnold | Scientific analysis of bartending — temperature, dilution, carbonation deep dives |
| **The Cocktail Companion** | Cheryl Charming | Deep cocktail history research |

## 3. YouTube Channels

| Channel | Style | Why Watch |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| **How to Drink** | Casual, humorous | Classic and creative recipes, clear and entertaining explanations |
| **Educated Barfly** | Professional teaching | One cocktail per episode, precise recipes, proper technique |
| **Anders Erickson** | Modern minimalist | Beautifully produced, practical recipes |
| **Steve the Bartender** | Australian style | Huge recipe library, systematic content |
| **Cocktail Chemistry** | Science-focused | Analyzes bartending from a scientific perspective |
| **Distinguished Spirits** | In-depth reviews | Spirit reviews and cocktail tutorials |
| **Vlad SlickBartender** | Fast-paced | Short video format, great for quick learning |
