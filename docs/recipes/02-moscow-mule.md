# Moscow Mule / 莫斯科骡子

**Difficulty / 难度:** ★☆☆☆☆

**Technique / 技法:** Build / 直调法

**Glassware / 杯具:** Copper mug or highball glass / 铜杯或高球杯

**Garnish / 装饰:** Lime wheel and fresh mint sprig / 青柠片和新鲜薄荷枝

---

## History / 背景

The Moscow Mule was invented in 1941 in New York City — the product of a clever marketing alliance. John G. Martin (who had excess Smirnoff vodka), Jack Morgan (who couldn't sell his ginger beer), and a woman with too many copper mugs joined forces. The iconic copper mug became the drink's signature, and the Moscow Mule is credited with kickstarting America's vodka craze.

莫斯科骡子诞生于 1941 年的纽约——源自一次巧妙的营销合作。约翰·G·马丁（手中有大量滞销的斯米诺伏特加）、杰克·摩根（卖不出去的姜汁啤酒）以及一位囤积了大量铜杯的女士联手推广了这款酒。标志性的铜杯成为了这款酒的经典形象，而莫斯科骡子也被认为是引爆美国伏特加热潮的功臣。

## Instructions / 步骤

### English

1. Fill a copper mug (or highball glass) with ice cubes.
2. Pour 60 ml (2 oz) of vodka over the ice.
3. Add 15 ml (0.5 oz) of freshly squeezed lime juice.
4. Top with chilled ginger beer. Pour gently to preserve the fizz.
5. Stir briefly to combine — just two or three gentle turns.
6. Garnish with a lime wheel and a sprig of fresh mint.
7. Serve immediately.

### 中文

1. 在铜杯（或高球杯）中装满冰块。
2. 将 60 ml（2 oz）伏特加倒在冰块上。
3. 加入 15 ml（0.5 oz）新鲜挤榨的青柠汁。
4. 缓慢加入冰镇姜汁啤酒至补满，轻柔倒入以保留气泡。
5. 简短搅拌混合——轻柔搅拌两到三圈即可。
6. 用青柠片和新鲜薄荷枝装饰。
7. 立即享用。

## Pro Tips / 进阶技巧

- **Ginger beer ≠ ginger ale / 姜汁啤酒 ≠ 姜汁汽水：** Ginger beer has a spicier, more robust flavor than ginger ale. Use real ginger beer for authentic results. 姜汁啤酒比姜汁汽水口感更辛辣浓烈，务必使用真正的姜汁啤酒。
- **The copper mug matters / 铜杯有讲究：** Copper conducts cold quickly, keeping the drink extra chilled and creating a pleasant tingling on the lips. 铜杯导冷性强，让酒保持更冰凉，触碰嘴唇时带来独特的冰爽感。
- **Fresh lime is non-negotiable / 必须用新鲜青柠：** Bottled lime juice tastes flat and artificial. Always squeeze fresh. 瓶装青柠汁口感寡淡，永远使用新鲜挤榨。
- **Try different ginger beers / 尝试不同品牌的姜汁啤酒：** Fever-Tree is clean and balanced; Bundaberg is sweet; Q Ginger Beer is dry and spicy. Each gives a different character. 不同品牌风格各异，多尝试找到自己的最爱。
